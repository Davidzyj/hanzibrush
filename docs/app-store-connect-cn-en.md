## English

App Name: Hanzi Brush

Subtitle: Chinese calligraphy practice

Promotional Text: Practice beautiful Chinese characters with guided brush tracing, cultural notes, favorites, and local artwork creation.

Description:
Hanzi Brush helps you discover the beauty of Chinese characters through calm daily practice. Learn one character at a time, read a short culture note, follow stroke hints, trace on a guided grid, and create simple Hanzi artwork cards.

Features:
- Daily Hanzi practice
- Guided brush tracing with grid and ghost character
- Culture notes for each character
- Favorites and practice progress stored locally
- Artwork templates for shareable character cards
- English, Simplified Chinese, and Japanese interface
- No account, no backend, no active data collection

Keywords:
hanzi, chinese, calligraphy, brush, mandarin, culture, writing, characters, chinese art, language
