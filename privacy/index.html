Hanzi Brush Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

English

Hanzi Brush is a local iPhone app. The app does not require an account, does not connect to a backend server, and does not actively collect personal data.

  • Practice progress, favorites, language preference, and artwork metadata are stored locally on your device.
  • The app opens the privacy policy, support page, or email app only when you explicitly tap those links.
  • The app does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, or third-party login.
  • If you email support, your email address and message are handled only to respond to your request.
